In order to claim a place, you just need to be able to answer a call to your place's phone number or upload an official document that shows your place's name and address.

You can claim any place with a physical presence, in over 1600 categories, from repair shops to schools to places of worship.

App Clips are a small part of an app that you can use to do somethign quickly, like rent a bike, pay for parking, or order food. On Apple Business Register, you can choose which App Clips that have been registered for your place can appear on your Place Card.

An App Clip is the best way for customers to discover your business's App Clips when they're at your store. Vistors can scan an App Clip Code to launch the App Clips experience of your choice.
